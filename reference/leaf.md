# Phylosphere samples

Plant microbiome samples from the phylosphere, taken from a paper by
Smets et al.

## Usage

``` r
leaf
```

## Format

### `leaf`

A tidytacos object with 6,044 taxa and 33 samples:

- Plant:

  plant species (Blank/Mustard/Wheat)

- Plot:

  location of the plant or blank

- Day:

  day of sampling

- added_spike_copies:

  amount of spike in sequences added
